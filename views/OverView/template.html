About this dataset

This is an interactive summary of the results of a survey that was deployed as part of a study into democratizing non-tabular data abstractions. With this exception of this Overview panel, responses are presented in the same context that participants saw each question.

Survey Version

Preliminary pilots of the survey (v0.1.0 and v0.1.1) were conducted at the University of Arizona. In addition to the University of Arizona campus community, v1.0.0 was deployed at the 2019 IEEE VIS and Supercomputing conferences.
